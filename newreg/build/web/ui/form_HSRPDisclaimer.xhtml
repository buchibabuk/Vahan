  • As per the MoRTH Notification S.O. 1018(E) dated 25-Feb-2019, A vehicle manufacturer shall comply with the following procedure in regard to High Security Registration Plates (HSRP) for the vehicles manufactured on or after 1st April, 2019 namely:-
  • (a) The type approved High Security Registration Plates including the third registration plate (sticker to be pasted on windscreen), shall be supplied by the vehicle manufacturers to their dealers, who shall place a mark of registration on such plates and affix them on the auto-mobiles.
